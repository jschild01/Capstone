1.  Only recently a man with both arms permanently 
disabled was called by his draft board and inducted. 
This somewhat angered him that he a man with no 
arms should be inducted, so on being assigned to his 
army post he asked his superior officer, what could 
he do. The officer pointed out a soldier drawing water 
from a well and pouring it into a large barrel. 
But the no-armed said to the officer "I can't do 
that because I do not have any arms. Well said 
the officers thats just where we can use you, be-
cause you see the soldier drawing water is blind 
and you can tell him when the barrel is full.
